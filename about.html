TAXONOMY
Derived from the Greek zoon (ζῴον), meaning "animal," and planktos (πλαγκτός), meaning "wanderer," Züplankta is a nonprofit, open-source repository of the world's most diverse architectural scale figures created by students at the Carnegie Mellon School of Architecture. We aim to highlight Earth's incredible biodiversity in order to draw into sharp focus the urgent need for ecological empathy in our current climate crisis. Good design respects and supports the complex network of life on our planet.
